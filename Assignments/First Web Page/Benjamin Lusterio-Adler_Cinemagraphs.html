Cinemagraphs

What Are Cinemagraphs?

Cinemagraphs are moving images, but not in the way that videos or GIFs are moving images. A cinemagraph should be an interesting or beautiful still image that is enhanced by movement. In a cinemagraph, the image is edited so that some parts of the picture stay perfectly still while other parts move around it.

How Do I Make a Cinemagraph?

There are many ways to make cinemagraphs. The most popular and effective method, however, is the use of Adobe Photoshop. to watch a video tutorial on how to make a cinemagraph using Adobe Photoshop, click here. As the cinemagraph medium grows in popularity, however, new and easier ways to make them surface.

Michael Jordan

History

Michael Jordan is arguably one of the best players to ever play in the NBA. Michael Jordan was born on February 17, 1963 in Brooklyn, New York. He played most of his career for the Chicago Bulls and then finished his career playing for the Washington Wizards. Michael had a successful career when he played, a list of some of the important titles are located in the list below. Jordan had his number 23 retired in the Bulls organization and the college he attended in North Carolina.

Important Tittles

  1. NBA champion
  2. NBA Finals MVP
  3. NBA Most Valuable Player
  4. NBA All-Star
  5. NBA All-Star Game MVP
  6. All-NBA First Team
